ABOUT US

Our story begins.......

Fab-Kiddies club was established in Rotterdam, Netherlands in the year 2022 by Esther Babatunde. In Fab Kiddies club, we strongly believe kids are extremely precious and they should be treated with so much love and nurturing, and that's why we came up with a kiddies club where kids can play while having fun. Before coming up with an idea of a kiddies club, we realised alot of kids only had a routine of going to school in the morning, get back home in the evening, eat and sleep at 7pm. The routine continues even on the weekend, well except the school aspect. We plan different activities every month for our Fab kids. For more information, kindly contact us on our contact us page.
